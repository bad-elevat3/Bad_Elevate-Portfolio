About Me

Business Systems & IT Graduate with a focus on bridging the gap between technology and business operations.

I specialize in understanding how people, processes, and technology intersect. My approach: ask the right questions, analyze the gaps, and deliver solutions that are not just technically sound, but truly usable and impactful.

Professional Background

I completed my Bachelor of Information Technology in Business Systems (2025), which equipped me with expertise in business analysis, systems design, data management, and web development. My professional experience spans multiple domains:

  • Internal IT Auditing - Ensuring compliance and operational efficiency
  • Database Management - Designing and optimizing data solutions
  • Business Reporting - Translating data into actionable insights
  • Web Development - Building functional, user-centered applications

I thrive in environments where documentation is incomplete and solutions must be figured out collaboratively. This has sharpened my problem-solving skills and ability to adapt to complex, ambiguous situations.

My Philosophy

Great solutions don't just emerge from technology—they come from understanding why processes exist, who they serve, and how they can be improved. I'm passionate about:

  • Understanding business objectives before jumping to technical solutions
  • Identifying systemic inefficiencies and root causes
  • Designing solutions that balance business needs with technical feasibility
  • Building systems that people actually want to use

Current Trajectory

I'm actively growing as a Business Analyst / Junior Developer, continuously sharpening both my technical and analytical capabilities. I'm seeking opportunities where I can:

  • Analyze complex business processes and identify improvement opportunities
  • Bridge communication between business stakeholders and technical teams
  • Contribute to the full software development lifecycle
  • Build solutions that drive measurable business impact
